THE PATH TO THE SCROLL

The selection pipeline is designed to eliminate those who cannot meet the Regiment's uncompromising standard.

8 WEEKS

RASP 1

The crucible. Physical and mental attrition. Candidates face relentless PT and land navigation.

8 WEEKS

RASP 2

Senior leader assessment. Tactical planning, mission briefs, and leadership under observation.

4 WEEKS

PRE-RANGER

Intensive internal program preparing candidates for Ranger School. Zero shortcuts.

MINIMUM ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

  • Minimum age requirement: 14+
  • Must hold rank of E-2 or higher within USAR
  • Must not be blacklisted by ASOC, 75th, or affiliated commands
  • Must possess prior Special Operations, reconnaissance, or tactical milsim experience
  • Must demonstrate maturity, professionalism, and discipline
  • Must maintain operational composure under pressure
  • Must be capable of functioning within a structured chain of command
  • Must possess clear and effective English communication skills
  • Must have a working microphone and reliable activity level
  • Must display initiative, adaptability, and willingness to improve
  • Must possess foundational tactical knowledge and battlefield awareness
  • Must be capable of operating effectively in small-team environments
  • Must successfully complete RASP and unit evaluation phases
  • Applicants with histories of misconduct or unit-hopping may be denied.

CANDIDATE EXPECTATIONS

Personnel selected for assessment are expected to uphold the standards of the Regiment at all times. Selection is based not only on skill, but consistency, professionalism, and teamwork.

FAILURE TO MAINTAIN STANDARDS MAY RESULT IN REMOVAL FROM PIPELINE.
